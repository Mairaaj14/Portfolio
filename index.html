Who is Maira?

Hello there, my name is Maira, I was born in Mexico City and raised in San Francisco California
since I was 10 years old. I graduated from George Washington High School in 2014 and continued my education at San Francisco State University to pursue a career in Pshychology and a minor in Latino Studies. Although I liked the career path I was taking I found myself not being too happy and decided to explore other majors. Computer Science had always been interesting to me because I watched my dad fix his own computers when I was a kid, he introduced me to all the video games I know and taught me my first keyboard shortcuts. After taking Intro to Computer Science at State they literally had me at "Hello World", at this moment I knew I wanted to be able to bring my ideas to life! I knew that if I got into coding I could make that happen. I decided to give my career a change and decided to dive into UC Berkeley's full time coding bootcamp to become a full stack web developer, and master in both the back end and the front end of web development.
